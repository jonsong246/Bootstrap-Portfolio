Hello! Welcome to my gallery. My name is Jonathan Song and I am currently a Senior 3D Animation Major at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York. I am originally from Orange County, California and I moved to the big city to further enhance my experience as a Digital Arts Student. Here you will find a collection of my work primarily constructed through various software including Autodesk Maya, Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, and Aftereffects. This is a composition of my projects throughout the course of my academic years here in New York. Through the creation of this webpage, I am able to showcase my work and hope to deliver some insight as well as receive constructive feedback. Thank you for visiting my page!
